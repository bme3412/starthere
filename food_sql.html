My post-Turkey day taper

Tracking calories and shedding pounds with the help of MySQL

December 31, 2021

I stepped on the scale a few days after Thanksgiving and saw a number I really did not like (if there was a betting line, take the over). So in order to improve my diet I used the nutrition app MyFitnessPal and MySQL to create a database to track all the meals I ate in the month of December. I also tracked all my exercise and weighed myself each day.

To start my journey to becoming healthier, and as a believer in science, first I consulted some nutrition calculators online for a desired daily calorie and nutrient goal. Ultimately, to reach my desired weight loss target of 30 lbs, I need to consume a total of 1,910 (net) calories per day, with my "macro" targets in a split of 50% carbohydrates (translates to 239 g), 30% fat (64 g), and 20% protein (96g).


For exercise, my target was to burn 1,890 calories throguh either 4 workouts per week or 4 hours of exercise total. My targets for micro-nutrients included: 21g saturated fat, 300 mg cholestorel, 2300 mg sodium, 3500 mg potassium, 38 g of fiber, 72g sugar, and 100% sugggested daily value consumption of Vitamin A, Vitamin C, calcium, and iron.
